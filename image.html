Difference between lossy and lossless compression in images

The difference between Lossy and Lossless compression is that Lossy compression takes away parts of the file that are not needed or noticed to make them smaller.

Why is it important for Web Designers to compress image files?

It is important to compress your images because if you have a large image it can make the loading of the website really long and then no one will want to go on your website

How compression effects the quality of the data

When you compress the data the quality only changes a little bit and you can't see much of a difference.
